My Story

Hello, I'm
Trang Luong

I'm a single mom with two amazing kids — and I consider myself a very genuine, easy-going, and kinda funny person. Photography has always been a passion of mine, but life put a temporary hold on it.

Now that my daughter is in college and my son is on his way, I am finally able to pursue what brings me immense joy. I know there are a million photographers out there — and yes, your fancy phone takes great shots too. But what I'm most excited about is my ability to capture your unique beauty in stunning, high-quality images right down to the last detail.

Whether it's a classic family session or a creative glamorous shoot, my goal is to bring out your best. I would love to share that special experience with you. Together we can create beautiful images that you will cherish for a lifetime.
